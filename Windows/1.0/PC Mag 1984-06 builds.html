PC Mag 1984-06 Tech Seminar Demonstration builds

(copied directly from wiki)

These OEM versions of a pre-release version of Windows 1.0 were demonstrated at a tech seminar and then written about in a June 1984 release of PC Mag:[1]

  • Altos
  • Bytes-Comterm
  • Columbia Data Products
  • Honeywell
  • ITT Qume
  • Polo Microsystems
  • TeleVideo
  • Texas Instruments

Of course, these could be a number of different Windows builds used, but the date of the PC Mag article would suggest that they are most likely based on DR1 or DR2.
